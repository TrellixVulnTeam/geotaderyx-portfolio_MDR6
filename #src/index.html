Creativity

Creativity is when you are given pieces of something trivial and dare to bind it with your "inner" glue. This is when the rules are the exception to your rules. This is when objectivity is tossed like dice in the hope of never seeing them fall.

Creative thinking is not a professional activity, but a way to create and strengthen a connection with life. Creativity is not just about painting a picture, writing a novel, or building a house. The main thing is the full awareness of his presence.
